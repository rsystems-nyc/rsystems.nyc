## What We Do
RSystems provides executive-level IT strategy, infrastructure design, and technology 
consulting to mid-market organizations. We engage at the C-suite level to define and 
scope initiatives, then work alongside internal IT teams or MSP partners for 
day-to-day implementation.

We handle infrastructure-level implementation — architecture, design, and the heavy 
lifting that requires senior technical expertise. We do not provide tier 1 support, 
managed services, or day-to-day operational work such as onboarding new hires. 
We build the onboarding systems and workflows; the client's team or MSP runs them.

## Ideal Client
A well-funded organization of 100+ people with existing IT coverage — whether an 
internal team or an MSP — and executive leadership that takes technology seriously, 
but hasn't yet found the right senior technical partner to translate business goals 
into infrastructure strategy.

They don't need someone to manage their helpdesk. They need someone who can sit across 
from their CFO, COO, CTO, or CIO, ask the right questions, and build a plan their 
team can execute. In many engagements, RSystems serves as a fractional CTO or CISO — 
providing the senior technical leadership the organization needs without the overhead 
of a full-time hire.

Technically, they operate in enterprise environments — Microsoft 365, Azure, Apple MDM, 
VMware, and on-premises or hybrid datacenter. They care about high availability and 
reliability, not just getting things working.

Engagements start at $5,000/month. Clients are NYC-based or have a meaningful 
New York presence.

- Clean, minimal, lots of whitespace

## Hard Rules
- Never use stock illustrations
- Never use generic IT imagery (people pointing at screens, stock server rooms)
- Always mobile-first
- Keep navigation minimal — no mega menus
- CTAs should drive toward scheduling a conversation, not a generic "contact us"
- All copy should speak to the strategic buyer, not the IT administrator
